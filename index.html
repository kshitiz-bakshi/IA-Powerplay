We Are IA PowerPlay

IA PowerPlay, backed by India Accelerator, is a gaming startup accelerator program that will provide support and resources to early-stage gaming companies to help them grow and succeed. It will offer mentorship, funding, networking opportunities, and access to industry experts and resources.

✔️ The gaming industry is constantly evolving, and a startup accelerator can help new gaming companies navigate the competitive landscape and overcome common challenges, such as funding and marketing.
✔️ This program can also facilitate connections between startups and investors, helping them to secure additional funding to further their growth..
